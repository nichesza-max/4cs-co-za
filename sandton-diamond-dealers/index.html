Short answer

Diamond dealers in Sandton: what is actually there and what is 15 minutes away.

Sandton is South Africa's financial centre and has a concentration of luxury retail. That means high-end jewellery stores. Stone-first specialist dealing is a different business, and most of it in Johannesburg happens in Bedfordview, a short drive from Sandton.

Use this rule

Do not judge one C alone. Read the certificate, inspect the actual stone, then decide whether beauty, budget, or resale confidence matters most.

01

What Sandton actually offers

Sandton City mall houses the Diamond Walk, a precinct of luxury jewellery retailers. These include international and local brands selling set jewellery, engagement rings, and watches. Some carry certified natural diamonds. The retail experience is polished and the price points reflect it. For a buyer who wants an already-set ring from a brand, Sandton delivers that well. For a buyer who wants to select a loose stone, compare cut data, and negotiate on the stone's certificate merits, the retail format has limits.

02

Specialist dealing in Bedfordview

Bedfordview, in the Ekurhuleni metro, sits roughly 15 minutes from Sandton CBD by car. It holds a cluster of specialist diamond dealers who focus on loose certified stones and estate diamonds. This is where most of the stone-first work in the JHB market happens. Prodiam is the established specialist here: SADPMR-aligned, 30-plus years operating, by-appointment at Suite F1W6, The Paragon, 1 Kramer Road.

03

Which buyer belongs where

If you want a branded ring from a retail store in a high-security mall, Sandton is the right destination. If you want to buy a certified loose diamond, sell a stone, or get an independent valuation without paying a retail premium, a Bedfordview specialist is the right destination. Many JHB buyers visit Sandton first, then make the short trip to Bedfordview before committing.

04

Getting from Sandton to Prodiam

From Sandton CBD, take the N3 east or use the M1 via the Grayston interchange, then follow Kramer Road into Bedfordview. The drive is typically 15 to 20 minutes in normal traffic. Direct answers

Common questions

Are there specialist stone dealers actually in Sandton?

A small number operate in the broader Sandton area, but the density of specialist stone-first dealers is higher in Bedfordview, which is a short drive away.

Is Diamond Walk in Sandton City worth visiting?

Yes, if you want to see a range of luxury jewellery in one precinct. For comparing loose certified stone data and negotiating on price, a specialist appointment will serve you better.

How far is Prodiam from Sandton?

Approximately 15 minutes by car in standard traffic. The address is Suite F1W6, The Paragon, 1 Kramer Road, Bedfordview.

Can I buy a loose certified natural diamond in Sandton?

Some Sandton retailers carry certified loose stones. For a wider selection of natural certified diamonds with detailed technical comparison, book with a Bedfordview specialist.

Does Prodiam offer viewings by appointment only?

Yes. Prodiam operates by appointment at its Bedfordview office. Contact them before travelling to confirm availability and to describe what you are looking for or selling.

What is the difference between a retail jeweller and a specialist dealer?

A retail jeweller typically sells set jewellery at fixed prices. A specialist dealer handles loose stones, discusses certificate data in depth, and often buys as well as sells. The service model is advisory rather than retail.
