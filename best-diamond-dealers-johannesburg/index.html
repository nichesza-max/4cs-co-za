01

Chain retail in Johannesburg

Sandton City, Hyde Park, and Eastgate Shopping Centre carry the major chain retailers: national mall-based jewellery chains. These retailers offer finished jewellery, in-store financing, brand warranties, and the convenience of mall-based shopping. They are legitimate sellers of real diamonds. If you want a designed ring, a gift piece, or a standard piece with known brand backing, the Sandton retail corridor is a practical destination.

02

Specialist dealers: Bedfordview and beyond

Bedfordview, 12 km east of Sandton, is where much of JHB's non-retail diamond trade operates. The trade here is stone-oriented: loose diamonds, certificate review, sourcing for jewellers, and private client consultations. Prodiam at The Paragon is by-appointment. a Cape Town diamond specialist is Cape Town-based but operates nationally for significant transactions. For buyers who want to discuss a stone's GIA certificate, its market position, and its trade value, Bedfordview is the zone.

03

When to choose a specialist over a chain

If you are buying a diamond above R50 000 and want to understand exactly what you are paying for, a specialist consultation before committing to a retail purchase gives you a reference point. If you are selling a diamond or need a valuation for insurance, a specialist dealer reads from the same trade benchmarks that actual buyers use.

Direct answers

Common questions

Where exactly is Prodiam in Johannesburg?

Prodiam is at Suite F1W6, The Paragon, 1 Kramer Road, Bedfordview, Johannesburg. Appointments are booked by calling +27 11 334 9010 or emailing sales@prodiam.co.za.

Are the diamond dealers in Johannesburg regulated?

SADPMR-registered dealers operate under the South African Diamond and Precious Metals Regulator's licensing framework. Ask any dealer for their SADPMR registration status before a significant transaction.

Can I sell my diamond to a Johannesburg dealer?

Yes. Prodiam and other specialist dealers assess and purchase certified natural diamonds. Bring the original GIA or equivalent grading report and the physical stone. Expect a trade-rate offer, not retail replacement value.

Is Bedfordview worth the drive from Sandton for a diamond purchase?

For buyers focused on stone quality, GIA certification, and trade pricing, yes. The 15-minute drive from Sandton gives access to a specialist market that operates differently from mall retail.

Do JHB chain stores carry GIA-certified diamonds?

Some do, some do not. Ask specifically. Many chain stones carry store-branded or third-party valuations rather than GIA reports. Verify the certificate type and check the report on the lab's official website.

What is the FICA requirement for buying a diamond in SA?

FICA, the Financial Intelligence Centre Act, requires that dealers conduct customer due diligence for high-value transactions. For significant diamond purchases or sales, expect to provide identity documents. This is a legal AML requirement, not a discretionary policy.
