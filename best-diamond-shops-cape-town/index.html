Do Cape Town chain stores sell genuine diamonds?

Yes. Chain retailers in Cape Town sell real diamonds. The distinction from specialists is emphasis: chains lead with design and brand, specialists lead with stone grades and certificate data.

Is the V and A Waterfront a good place to buy a diamond?

For finished jewellery and a comfortable retail experience, yes. For a stone-first, certificate-led purchase of a significant diamond, supplement the retail visit with a specialist consultation.

How do I get a diamond appraised in Cape Town?

A qualified independent valuer or a specialist dealer like a Cape Town diamond specialist can appraise a stone. For a second opinion or trade-benchmark valuation, Prodiam in Johannesburg offers consultations by appointment.

What is the best way to find a trusted diamond jeweller in Cape Town?

Check for South African Jewellery Council membership, ask whether they can supply or mount GIA-certified stones, and request references for custom commission work. The V and A Waterfront has several reputable independent jewellers alongside chain stores.
